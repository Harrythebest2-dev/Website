What is Eating Habits?

Eating habits refer to the conscious, collective, and repetitive behaviors an individual or group follows regarding the selection, preparation, and consumption of food. These habits define what, how, when, and why people eat, influenced by social, cultural, and economic factors, ultimately impacting health and well-being.

How Can I Have A Good Eating Habit?

To establish good eating habits, focus on consuming a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while reducing intake of sugar, salt, and saturated fats. Key strategies include mindful eating (eating slowly, minimizing distractions), planning meals in advance, staying hydrated with water, and controlling portion sizes.
