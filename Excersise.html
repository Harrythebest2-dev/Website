What is Excerise?

id="home_box">

Exercise is a type of physical activity that involves planned, structured, and repetitive bodily movement done to maintain or improve one or more components of physical fitness.

There are four main types of exercise—aerobic (cardio), strength (resistance), flexibility, and balance—which together improve cardiovascular health, muscle strength, joint mobility, and stability. Other forms include HIIT for efficiency, agility training, and mobility work. A well-rounded routine incorporates elements from all categories for optimal health.

  1. Regular exercise is crucial for a healthy, long life, reducing the risk of major illnesses like heart disease, stroke, type 2 diabetes, and cancer by up to 30% to 35%. It strengthens muscles and bones, controls weight, improves mental health by reducing anxiety and depression, and enhances brain function and sleep quality.
